How it works

Darken the MacBook screen only when it is duplicating another display.

DisplayHelperPro is made for desks, demos, and presentation setups where the built-in screen keeps glowing even though the external display is the one you are actually using.

Privacy & safety

No data collection. No analytics. No tracking.

DisplayHelperPro's display-management features run locally on your Mac. The app does not collect, store, sell, share, or transmit personal information, and update checks are used only to look for new versions. Feedback menu items open a draft in your email app; nothing is sent unless you choose Send.

Read the full privacy policy
What customers should know

A small utility with a deliberately small footprint.

No account There is no sign-in, subscription account, or cloud service.
No analytics The app does not track usage, events, clicks, sessions, or installs.
No automatic reporting Feedback opens an email draft, and logs are copied only when you choose to copy them.
Local preferences only macOS may store settings such as enabled state and restore brightness locally.
